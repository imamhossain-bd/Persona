    Persona has proudly been one of the pioneers of beauty related services in Bangladesh since it was first established in 1998 and has since come a long and

    over 1,70,00,000 of Persona’s premium services. The high-quality services and achievements haven’t been bound to its 11 ladies beauty outlets only, but have

    array of expertise, aims to enhance beauty using each and every square foot of the total 1,07,148 square feet made available to us. However, enhancing
    beauty isn’t an easy task, and hence Persona has invested in multiple sister concerns apart from the ladies and men’s beauty outlets including Persona Health,
    Persona Academy, Spring Spa and Canvas Magazine to cater to the beauty, fashion and health needs of all of the precious clients with utmost sincerity.
